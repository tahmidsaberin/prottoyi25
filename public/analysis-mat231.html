📐 Mathematics

Mathematical Analysis

A rigorous treatment of real analysis — the foundation beneath calculus. We explore the logic of limits, continuity, differentiation, integration, sequences, and series with full mathematical precision.

🎓 MAT 231
📊 3 Credits
📅 Even Semester
🏛️ Dept. of Mathematics, CU
📋 Course Syllabus 9 Topics
1
The Real Number System
Axioms, order, completeness, Archimedean property, supremum and infimum
Done
2
Sequences of Real Numbers
Convergence, divergence, Cauchy sequences, subsequences, Bolzano-Weierstrass
Done
3
Limits and Continuity
ε-δ definitions, limit theorems, one-sided limits, uniform continuity
Done
4
Differentiability
Derivative definition, chain rule, mean value theorems, L'Hôpital's rule
In Progress
5
Taylor's Theorem & Series
Taylor's theorem, Maclaurin series, remainder estimation, power series
Upcoming
6
Riemann Integration
Riemann sums, integrability criteria, fundamental theorem of calculus
Upcoming
7
Series of Real Numbers
Convergence tests: ratio, root, integral, alternating series, absolute convergence
Upcoming
8
Sequences & Series of Functions
Pointwise and uniform convergence, Weierstrass M-test, power series
Upcoming
9
Metric Spaces (Introduction)
Open/closed sets, compactness, connectedness, completeness
Upcoming
