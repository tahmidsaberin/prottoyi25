Who We Are

Built by Students,
For Students

Prottoyi 25 is a community-driven platform created by students of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Chittagong.

Our mission is simple — transform scattered past year questions into structured, strategic intelligence so every student walks into the exam hall prepared, not surprised.

📊
Data-Driven Analysis
Every insight is tied directly to actual PYQs — no vague advice, only frequency data and patterns.
🎯
Strategic Roadmaps
Know exactly what to study first, what to skip, and how to allocate your 48 hours before the exam.
🤝
Community Contribution
Every student can upload their analysis. Together we build the most comprehensive exam prep resource at CU CSE.
